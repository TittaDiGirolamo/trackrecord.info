How it works

A transparent, human-reviewed process

1
Public prediction is made
A forecaster shares a clear, falsifiable prediction publicly (on X, in media, podcasts, or official forecasts) with a verifiable source and time horizon.
2
Logged & prepared
Trackrecord.info records the prediction with source, timestamp, and resolution criteria. It is added to the public dataset as “Pending – Resolution-ready”.
3
Human review
Every submission and resolution is reviewed by humans for verifiability, independence, and adherence to the methodology before publication.
4
Outcome resolved
On or after the expected date, a human reviewer confirms the actual result from primary/official sources and records it with rationale.
5
Scored & published
A transparent 0–100 accuracy score is calculated. Reports and forecaster metrics update automatically and remain permanently public.

Full details in METHODOLOGY.md. Everything is designed for transparency and repeatability.
